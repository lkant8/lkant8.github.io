# 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide, improve, secure, and maintain the Services.

## 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

HealthEvo is designed to function primarily offline.

We do not require users to create an account and we do not intentionally collect or store personal information on our servers during normal app usage.

Any health or wellness data entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device unless the user explicitly chooses to export, back up, or share data using device-supported features.

Depending on optional features enabled by the user, limited information may be processed locally on-device, such as:

- Height
- Weight
- BMI
- Fitness goals
- Workout information
- Nutrition tracking
- Sleep tracking
- Wellness data

We do not sell or remotely store this information.

## 2.2 Health & Sensitive Personal Information

HealthEvo may process sensitive health-related information locally on your device when you voluntarily use wellness or tracking features.

Examples may include:

- Height
- Weight
- BMI
- Diet information
- Fitness activity
- Sleep information
- Workout information
- Nutrition data

This information is:

- Stored locally on your device
- Not transmitted to HealthEvo servers during normal app usage
- Not sold to third parties
- Accessible only to you unless you explicitly share or export it

If optional integrations or cloud features are added in the future, explicit user consent will be obtained before any data transmission occurs.
